Well, at least we have a question that can be answered with relative certainty. And it looks like nobody is prepared to provide a direct answer the question asked at the beginning of this thread, who is John Titor?.

So I would like to take advantage of the opportunity to compare Batman to the John Titor story.

We can definitely answer the question of who the Batman is by some quick research. According to Wikipedia, Batman is a fictional character, a comic book superhero created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger in 1939.

If you are asking who the Batman is in the story itself, the answer is Bruce Wayne. We know this because Kane and Finger introduced all of the characters from the storys inception many years ago and published it for all to read in the form of comic books. None of that information was hidden.

We can also answer the question by listing each actor who played the role of Batman in each movie that has been produced.

Now, consider for the moment if Kane and Finger decided to pull of a publicity stunt before they released their first comic book. So they figure out how to disguise someone as the Batman character and fly him around the city with a well-hidden jet pack. Of course, they would need a large search light with the classic bat symbol to easily attract attention.

If this is pulled of properly, there is no doubt that some people would believe there is a really Batman flying around while more skeptic people would quickly declare it a hoax. But one thing we can be assured of is that everyone would want to know who the Batman really is not matter if he was legit or a hoax.

Of course, there would be those who have inside information of the truth but may have been sworn to secrecy by some very menacing thugs who promised serious reprisals if they spoke out.

And these people would naturally declare that the whole thing was a hoax buy they are not allowed to say how they know. And everyone else without the benefit of this inside information would always wonder who the heck the Batman character is.

So I propose that Batman and Titor as similar in that respect except that we know for sure that John Titor (time traveler or not) was a real person. So allow me to rephrase the question initially asked at the beginning of this thread:
