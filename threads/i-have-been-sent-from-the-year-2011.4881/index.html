I have been sent from the year 2011. It has long been rumored that this forum was visited before by another time-traveler. I hope my attempts to warn you are not futile, for I realize that it is hard to believe someone who makes a claim to have come from the future. In the year 2009, there will be a terrible earthquake hundreds of miles out in the pacific ocean. I cannot divulge exactly where it will take place due to the distortion it may cause, but a tsunami wave will envelope highly populated areas in the south-pacific. The death count will reach one-million. I hope I have not said too much, but even if I have, perhaps this event will not occur.

I know you must be curious about my journey, but I know nothing about the machine that brought me here. I am a Major in the United States Air Force. I was sent back to an office I worked at in Amarillo, Texas to relay this message to you. Please, take precautions in the South Pacific. To lose a million souls in the course of 72 hours is such a terrible tragedy that will change the course of the world and spark major, equally awful events. I only have time to await for one reply to know that my message has been received. Please, take precautions.
