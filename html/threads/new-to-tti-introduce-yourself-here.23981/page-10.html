So I saw the thing about my posts needing to be approved. I don't usually introduce myself, because I don't like the idea of explicitly stating anything about me when there's no real need. But to bump up my posts (to get to the full member status) I figured I'd make one. So here it goes.


I'm Kafke, I've used this alias pretty much my entire duration of being online. I can't remember quite when I started using it, probably around 2003 or so. It's a portmanteau of two character names: Kafei (from Zelda Majora's Mask), and Sasuke from the Naruto anime series. It's irrelevant now, as it's been many years since I've been into Naruto. But I'm still a fan of Zelda, and it's Time travel stuff.


I've been into time travel ever since I've learned of it. Along with all things paranormal. I've grown to be a natural skeptic, mainly from my disappointments with experiments in such dealings. As such, I have a high bar for tolerance of "fakes". So please do expect that anything I write here I seriously believe, and don't intend to mislead anyone. I have a post I'd like to make in the "I'm a time traveler" forum, despite me not actually being a time traveler (though I do think I have direct experience with multiple timelines and changes made by time travelers). More on that in a minute.


I've known about TimeTravelInstitute.com for a while, after learning about it while reading up on John Titor (who I'd actually found out about through an anime called Steins;Gate). And I figured now is as good of time as any to actually make an account here. There seems to be no other active and long-lived time travel communities, so I'd end up here sooner or later. Up until now, I've mostly been on Reddit, and it's /r/TimeTravel board. Hopefully this place is as good as I hope.


Throughout my dealings with Time Travel logistics and fiction, I believe I've come up with a model that should account for any possible dealings with both real and fictional time travel, as well as reveal what is and isn't possible in these scenarios. It's still a WIP and doesn't really account for things I've recently had experience with.


Finally, as I mentioned, I do believe I've had direct interaction with time travel (though not have actually time traveled myself, unless potentially a second back in time counts ). Though I do have "bursts" of deja vu, which I've come to believe are an indicator changes in the timeline are taking place. Kind of like a real life reading;steiner. This is the topic which I wish to address, and is my main reason for signing up an account here.


I believe I have a sort of reading;steiner ability that allows me to detect changes that have occurred in the past, and alterations made to the world that go largely undetected. I believe a few other individuals have this ability as well, and I'll outline my observations here:


There are clear events in history that I remember, that don't line up to modern observation. This is a direct change. These changes take place at certain times. And I believe I have found a way to determine when they are about to come. My prediction is that we'll observe such a change at the end of this month (exciting!).


So before I get into it, I'd like to ask whether or not I should indeed post this over in the "I'm a time traveler" board. Since I'm not sure whether it counts (I don't claim to be a time traveler). But I do believe that you guys would be interested in what I have to say.


Either way, I look forward to having discussions and interacting with the people here. You guys seem to be my kind of people.
