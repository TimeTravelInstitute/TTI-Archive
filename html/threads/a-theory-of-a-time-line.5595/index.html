Suppose you could travel through the dimension of time without using a time machine. Perhaps through Astral Projection, or even through dreams!

Speaking of dreams. I dreamed that an unnamed individual came to me. He revealed the truth to everything. He answered all my questions. Showed me how the universe was made, and how it works.

Unfortunately, like most dreams they fade in my waking moment. Dreams turn out like distant memories over a matter of minutes. Although, there are a few things I remember, and here they are.

1. An individual timeline, or subjective experience, lies within his or her DNA. A simplified version of their life code is printed in flesh; your fingerprint. With the right technology an individual, or organisation, could determine the path you are currently on.

Is it not compulsary for every U.S. citizen, at some point, to supply a fingerprint record of themselves to the State.

2. The entire physical and material construct of the universe is, in fact, an illusion. A holographic prison where we act as our own warden. We are the jury, judge, and executioners of our own fortune.

3. In this fabric of dimensional existence the world works like clock-work. When this "clock" has made one complete turn a dimensional gateway opens which allows the passage to ultimate freedom.

December 21, 2012?

4. Though it may appear that we move through time, as we witness decay and change, we are merely skimming through dimensions of possibility. Skipping through the layers of infinity. There is no past, there is no present, there is no future.

Those who travel through time are going through stargates that lead them to an alternate dimensional reality. They have gotten one step closer to learning the code of existence, yet at the same time remaining prisoners to this holographic universe.

5. Our divine selves are all connected and though it may seem like you are limited to just this one existence you are in fact experiencing everything simultaneously. You, as I am, are connected through the infinite realms, and infinite dimensions. We are One. We are everything.
