In a word, no. For not-so-ordinary humans, like physicists, the year 2508 won't be much better.

The problem isn't as much a matter of not being able to time travel as it is a matter of having no viable theory of time travel. There is no current theory of physics based on the "real" world around us that says time travel, other than relativistic time travel to the future (Special Relativity), is possible.

In other words, aside from theories that depend on unrealistic circumstances idealized specifically for the theory, we don't know if time travel is even possible in this universe.
